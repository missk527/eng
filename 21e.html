The Pros and Cons of Social Media

Video link: https://youtu.be/Hs5It2WmzMs

Notes

Pros of Social Media

  1. Global connectivity – Helps people stay in touch with friends, family, and communities worldwide.
  2. Easy access to information – News, tutorials, and educational content are available instantly.
  3. Business and career opportunities – Supports marketing, networking, freelancing, and personal branding.
  4. Platform for self-expression – Users can share ideas, creativity, and opinions freely.
  5. Social awareness and activism – Raises awareness about social issues and mobilizes support for causes.

❌ Cons of Social Media

  1. Addiction and time waste – Excessive scrolling can reduce productivity and real-life interaction.
  2. Mental health impact – Can contribute to anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem due to comparison culture.
  3. Privacy and security risks – Personal data may be misused or exposed.
  4. Spread of misinformation – Fake news and rumors can circulate quickly.
  5. Cyberbullying and online harassment – Negative behavior online can seriously affect emotional well-being.
