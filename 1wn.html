Topic: The impact of social media on Teenagers

Notes

  1. Mental Health Effects – How excessive use may lead to anxiety, depression, or low self-esteem.
  2. Social Connectivity – The positive side of connecting with friends, communities, and support groups.
  3. Cyberbullying Risks – Exposure to harassment, trolling, and online bullying.
  4. Body Image Issues – Unrealistic beauty standards and comparison culture fueled by curated content.
  5. Academic Performance – Distractions, procrastination, and reduced focus on studies.
  6. Sleep Disruption – Late-night scrolling and blue light affecting sleep cycles.
  7. Identity Formation – Influence on self-expression, personal identity, and peer pressure.
  8. Exposure to Misinformation – Risks of fake news, harmful challenges, and misleading content.
  9. Creativity & Opportunities – Platforms for showcasing talents, learning skills, and career opportunities.
  10. Addiction & Screen Time – The compulsive need to check notifications and its long-term behavioral impact.
