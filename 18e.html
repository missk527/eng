How to achieve work-life balance

Video link: https://youtu.be/97nvFZT5CTs

Notes
  1. Set clear boundaries between work time and personal time to avoid overworking.
  2. Prioritize tasks effectively by focusing on what is most important and urgent.
  3. Manage time wisely using schedules, planners, or digital tools.
  4. Learn to say no to excessive commitments that cause stress and burnout.
  5. Take regular breaks during work to refresh your mind and improve productivity.
  6. Maintain a healthy lifestyle through exercise, proper sleep, and balanced nutrition.
  7. Use technology smartly and avoid checking work emails outside working hours.
  8. Spend quality time with family and friends to strengthen personal relationships.
  9. Pursue hobbies and interests outside work to relax and recharge mentally.
  10. Practice self-care and stress management through meditation, mindfulness, or relaxation techniques.
