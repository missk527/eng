The role of art in society

Video link: https://youtu.be/zX_JhLejgmg

Notes
  1. Expression of Human Emotions – Art allows people to express feelings, ideas, and experiences that words alone cannot fully capture.
  2. Cultural Identity and Heritage – Art reflects a society’s traditions, beliefs, and history, helping preserve cultural heritage across generations.
  3. Social Awareness and Criticism – Art highlights social issues such as inequality, injustice, war, and environmental problems, encouraging public discussion.
  4. Communication and Connection – Art acts as a universal language that connects people beyond barriers of language, religion, or nationality.
  5. Education and Learning – Art enhances creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence, making learning more engaging and meaningful.
  6. Economic Contribution – Art and creative industries generate employment, tourism, and economic growth in society.
  7. Inspiration and Innovation – Art inspires new ideas and innovation in fields like technology, architecture, fashion, and design.
  8. Emotional Healing and Well-being – Art therapy and creative activities help reduce stress, heal trauma, and improve mental health.
  9. Reflection of Social Change – Art documents historical moments and reflects changes in society, values, and lifestyles over time.
  10. Entertainment and Enjoyment – Art provides pleasure, relaxation, and enjoyment, enriching everyday life and leisure.
