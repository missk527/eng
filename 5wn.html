Topic: How to OvercomeExamStress

Notes

  1. Plan and Organize Early – Create a realistic study timetable to avoid last-minute cramming.
  2. Break Study Sessions into Chunks – Use techniques like the Pomodoro method to stay focused without burnout.
  3. Prioritize Sleep – Ensure proper rest, as lack of sleep increases anxiety and reduces memory retention.
  4. Eat Healthy and Stay Hydrated – Balanced nutrition and water intake keep the brain energized.
  5. Practice Relaxation Techniques – Deep breathing, meditation, or yoga can calm nerves.
  6. Stay Physically Active – Regular exercise reduces stress hormones and boosts concentration.
  7. Avoid Comparisons with Others – Focus on your own preparation instead of competing or comparing.
  8. Practice Past Papers and Mock Tests – Builds confidence and familiarity with exam patterns.
  9. Stay Positive and Use Affirmations – Replace negative thoughts with encouraging self-talk.
  10. Take Short Breaks and Reward Yourself – Rewarding progress keeps motivation high and stress low.
